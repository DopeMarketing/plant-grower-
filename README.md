# Plant grower. 

## Overview
A simple plant care tracking app that helps users monitor and log essential care requirements for their houseplants. Users can track sunlight exposure, watering schedules, and soil conditions to maintain healthy plants.

## Problem
Plant owners struggle to remember when they last watered plants, whether plants are getting adequate sunlight, and what soil conditions look like, leading to plant neglect or overcare

## Target User
Indoor plant enthusiasts and beginners who want to develop consistent plant care habits

## Features
- Add and manage individual plants
- Log watering events with timestamps
- Track sunlight exposure hours/intensity
- Monitor soil condition status
- Set care reminders and notifications
